2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos and Images

What we collect: When you use the App, you provide photos of your home or living spaces for AI-powered decoration visualization.

How we use it: Your photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing to generate decorated versions of your space.

Data retention: We do NOT store your photos. Images are processed in real-time and immediately deleted after the decorated version is generated and returned to you. The processed images are only stored on your device if you choose to save them to your photo library.

What we collect: If you choose to unlock unlimited generations, we collect your email address.

How we use it:

  • To verify and unlock unlimited generations in the App

  • To communicate with you about service-related matters

Data retention: Email addresses are stored securely and retained until you request deletion.

2.3 Device Information

What we collect: We may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

How we use it: To improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand how users interact with the App.

2.4 Camera and Photo Library Access

Permissions required: The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to:

  • Take photos of your space directly in the App

  • Save generated images to your photo library

How we use it: Camera and photo library access is used solely for the purposes described above. We never access your photos without your explicit action (taking a photo or selecting one).
