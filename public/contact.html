Frequently Asked Questions

How does HolidayHome AI work?

HolidayHome AI uses Google's advanced AI technology to analyze your room photo and add realistic Christmas decorations. Simply upload a photo, choose your style, and watch as your space is transformed with festive decorations.

How many free designs do I get?

You start with 3 free designs. You can earn more free designs by sharing your referral code with friends - both you and your friend get +3 designs when they sign up using your code.

What types of rooms work best?

Well-lit photos of living rooms, bedrooms, dining rooms, and home exteriors work best. The AI works with any residential space, including kitchens, entryways, and even cozy corners.

Can I use my own custom decoration style?

Yes! Select "Custom Style" and describe exactly what you want - the AI will create decorations based on your description.

Are my photos stored anywhere?

No! We prioritize your privacy. Your photos are processed through the AI and immediately deleted. We never store your images on our servers.

Do you share my data with third parties?

Your photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing and are not retained by Google. We only use device IDs to track generation limits, and no personal information is collected.

How do the product recommendations work?

The AI analyzes the decorated image and suggests actual Christmas decorations you can buy. These are affiliate links to Amazon, and we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase.

Is the app available on Android?

Currently, HolidayHome AI is only available on iPhone. We're working on an Android version for the future.
